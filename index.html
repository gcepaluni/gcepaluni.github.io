Gabriel Cepaluni

I am an Associate Professor in the School of Humanities and Social Sciences at UNESP. My research focuses on comparative political economy, particularly in developing countries. I use experimental and quasi-experimental methods to study racial bias, inequality, and public policy outcomes.
